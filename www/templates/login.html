Rayyan QCRI is a 100% FREE web/mobile application to help systematic review authors screen citations in a quick, easy and enjoyable fashion. With this mobile app, you can download full reviews then screen them offline while you are riding the bus. Once connected, the app will automatically sync back to the Rayyan servers!
